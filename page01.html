"Design@UBALT"

This project was for school. We were placed in teams and each group was assigned with redesiging a widget on the "Design@UBALT" webpage. We ultimately redesigned the entire interface that would help new and enrolled students to select a design program that best suits their needs based on what questions they answer on the newly designed site.

My role consisted of researching, sketching, creating a user persona. Once fleshed out, we brought our design to life with Adobe XD.

Throughout this program, I've learned that designing not only enhances the visual aesthetics but also fosters empathy and user-centered thinking among UX/UI and junior developers, making them (me) more attuned to the end-users' needs and preferences.

Technologies Used:

  • Figma
  • Adobe XD
  • Google Slides
  • Miro
  • Canva
