"House of REM"

This project was fun because I learned how to place an image that will result it in looking like it is popping out from the page. I love designing the fine details, and I love a clean and minimal design! "House of REM" was a name that I came up with because I was listening to Ariana Grande at the time lol. Since the pandemic, online shopping has skyrockted for just about everyone. This concept was easy!

This project was more leisure and to help build my portfolio so my role encompassed the entire design process.

I learned that by applying a holistic approach to product development, that will lead to a more intuitive and enjoyable digital experiences, ultimately improving the quality and effectiveness of my work.
